About me

Hello, I am Gautier Vicat, and I am in the first year of the BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions) program at Montbéliard. This website is a project centered around the theme "Réplique!" It showcases various artistic creations such as films, books, or music that have been presented, all revolving around the same subject defined by the quote featured on the homepage. For my site, the initial theme is war, but I presented this theme to address different perspectives on it, including those of soldiers, reporters, and outsiders. If you wish to contact me, you can do so via the Contact Us page. You can also reach me through my social networks available in the footer or on the Contact Us page.
